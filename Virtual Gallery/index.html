Welcome to my virtual gallery using p5.js.

This site will bring you through three different sketches. All pieces include some form of rotations

and feature a pink and purple aesthetic theme. Each piece is animated and interactive, allowing users to change

the visuals, or move the camera position. The inspiration for these sketches came from space and mathematics.

Space is expressed in the colour themes and mysterious visuals. The spirograph reminded me most of space

as it uses many different points to form an interesting visual, similar to how many stars form galaxies. The theme of

mathematics is expressed through the geometric, structured and continuous movements of the sketches

that follow a set equation. All sketches are a bit chaotic yet unified.

Enjoy Space Rotations! :)
